Summary

Ajemba Mmesomachukwu Janefrances Nee Adoro was born on 9th of February, 1995 to the family of Mr and Mrs Anthony Chkwukelo Adoro. She is a native of Isulo in Orumba South Local Government Area of Anambra State and currently married to Eziowelle in Idemili North Local Government Anambra State. She is married to Dr Micheal Ajemba, a consultant in family medicine.
